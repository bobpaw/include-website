About Us

Adison Lampert

Founder, Director

Adison Lampert is a senior at the Liberal Arts and Science Academy. She has been programming since 8th grade and plans on pursuing Artificial Intelligence when she is older. As someone interested in AI, she knows how important diversity is to the industry of computer science. After many years of observing a diminishing female population in her classes, she couldn’t help but ask herself why. That’s when the idea for def include() was born. She hopes that her experience and knowledge about the challenges faced by middle school and high school girls in computer science can help girls like her to feel more confident and comfortable in their classes. Aside from programming, Adison loves to write and learn about history.
